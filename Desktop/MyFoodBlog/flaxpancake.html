Flaxseed Pancakes



I adapted this recipe from https://www.slenderkitchen.com/recipe/flaxseed-meal-pancakes-low-carb.

These pancakes are low carb and dairy free! Boxed mixes almost always contain dairy in them. I've tried multiple versions of pancakes that are attempting to be healthier but a lot of them don't turn out the consistency I like. I found this recipe to be exactly what I was looking for! I topped my pancakes with sliced strawberries, almond butter and honey.

Flaxseed Pancakes


Prep Time Cook Time Total Time Servings

5 min     10 min    15 min    2

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flaxseed meal
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp + 2 tsp unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp honey
  • sprinkle of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix together all the ingredients in a bowl. If it is too thick, add more almond milk or water.
  2. Heat 1tbsp of coconut oil in skillet over medium heat. Once hot, pour about 1/4 cup batter for each pancake. Let it cook until bubbles begin to form on top of the pancake. Once the bubbles begin to pop, flip the pancake and cook an additional 2-3 minutes.
  3. Top with your favorite toppings.
