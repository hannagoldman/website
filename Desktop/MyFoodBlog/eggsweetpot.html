Fried Egg on Sweet Potato Toast



This is my go-to breakfast almost every morning. It has a good mix of healthy fats, protein, and carbs to keep me full until lunch. I make sure to fry my eggs in a cast-iron skilet so that they get extra crispy on the bottom.

Fried Egg on Sweet Potato Toast


Prep Time Cook Time Total Time Servings

15 min     15 min    30 min    1

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • 1 tbsp avocado oil
  • Trader Joe's 'Everything But the Bagel Seasoning'

Instructions

  1. Slice the sweet potato into 1 inch thick slices.
  2. Stick the sweet potato slices into either a toaster or toaster oven at 400 degrees. If you use a toaster, you will probably have to press down and heat up the slices a couple of times. Continue cooking until the sweet potato is soft and slightly charred.
  3. While the sweet potato is cooking, heat up your avocado oil in a skillet on medium-high heat. Once hot, crack your eggs into the skillet. Cook until egg whites ae heated through but before the yolks cook. I usually put a lid on the skillet for the last 30 seconds of cooking to guarntee the egg whites are cooked on top.
  4. Once everything is done cooking, add the sweet potato to your plate with the fried eggs on top. Sprinkle 'Everything But the Bagel Seasoning' on top.
